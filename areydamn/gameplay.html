HOW TO PLAY

Type an anime title to make a guess. You have 10 HP. If your HP hits 0, it's GAME OVER.

  • Green = Exact Match
  • Yellow = Close Match
  • ↑ / ↓ Arrows = Target is higher/lower

Clues Unlocked as you lose HP:

Progressive Blur: The target image becomes clearer with every wrong guess.

Letter Skeleton: Letters of the title will randomly unlock at your 3rd, 6th, and 9th guess.
