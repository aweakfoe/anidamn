10 Interesting Anime Facts
01

Spirited Away is the only anime film in history to win an Academy Award (Best Animated Feature in 2003).

02

The highest-grossing anime film of all time is Demon Slayer: Mugen Train, earning over $500 million worldwide.

03

Over 60% of the world's animation-based entertainment is anime.

04

Sazae-san is the longest-running animated TV series in the world, with over 7,500 episodes since 1969.

05

The name Studio Ghibli comes from the Arabic word for "Mediterranean wind".

06

The titans in Attack on Titan are based on a drunk person the creator, Hajime Isayama, encountered in an internet cafe.

07

One Piece holds the Guinness World Record for the most copies published for the same comic book series by a single author.

08

The first anime to be broadcast in the United States was Astro Boy in 1963.

09

Death Note was briefly banned in parts of China because students were buying notebooks and writing their teachers' names in them.

10

The massive Pizza Hut product placements in Code Geass were part of a real-life marketing deal in Japan.
